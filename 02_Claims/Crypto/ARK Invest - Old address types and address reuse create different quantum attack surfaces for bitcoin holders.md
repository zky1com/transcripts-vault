# ARK Invest - Old address types and address reuse create different quantum attack surfaces for bitcoin holders

## Position
The speakers distinguish between always-exposed legacy wallets and conditionally exposed reused addresses in quantum-risk modeling.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript separates older key-address wallets from reused-address wallets in vulnerability analysis.
- Legacy wallets are described as exposing public keys by default.
- Reused addresses are described as becoming vulnerable once a spend reveals the public key.

## Logic Chain
1. Different wallet patterns reveal key material at different times.
2. Exposure timing changes attack probability and urgency of migration.
3. Thus mitigation policy should account for both legacy and reuse-driven risk pools.
