# Chamath Palihapitiya - Stablecoins provide dollar-pegged inflation protection for 500 million users in emerging markets

## Position

For users in countries like Kenya, where local currencies depreciate 60% annually, a dollar-pegged stablecoin in a crypto wallet preserves purchasing power at negligible cost.
