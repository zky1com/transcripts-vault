# ARK Invest - Quantum exposure could threaten ownership security for a large share of existing bitcoin supply

## Position
The episode argues quantum-capable key recovery could expose a material portion of bitcoin to theft risk under current address behaviors.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript states about one-third of supply (around 6.7 million BTC) is currently in quantum-vulnerable conditions.
- Discussion explains public-key exposure enables potential reverse-engineering of private keys in a quantum scenario.
- Panel calls this exposure potentially existential for network trust if unaddressed.

## Logic Chain
1. If attackers can derive private keys from revealed public keys, ownership can be compromised.
2. A large vulnerable share increases systemic theft and confidence risk.
3. Therefore quantum migration planning is a first-order security issue for Bitcoin.
