# John Collison - Stablecoins are most compelling today for cross-border payments and dollar access outside the US

## Position
John Collison says the strongest stablecoin use cases today are cross-border money movement and access to dollar balances outside the United States.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the interesting stablecoin use cases are "cross border" and "outside the US."
- He points to people in other countries wanting to hold dollar balances.
- He uses Nigeria's currency devaluation as an example of why the demand exists.

## Logic Chain
1. John identifies where current payment pain is highest.
2. He ties stablecoin demand to both remittances and dollar savings demand.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view that international use cases lead stablecoin adoption.
