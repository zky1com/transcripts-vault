# Kyle Samani - Modern blockchains like Solana can process more than one billion transactions per day at an aver

## Position
Solana's throughput of 1B+ transactions/day at <$0.01 average cost crosses the threshold where blockchain infrastructure can support global internet-scale capital markets — a capability that did not exist on earlier chains like Ethereum.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "modern blockchains like Salana can actually process now more than a billion transactions in a day at an average cost of less than a penny per transaction"
- Transcript: "No one in this room is going to wait two minutes to send the transaction and pay $20 in gas fees on Ethereum"
- Transcript: "The tech is finally ready"

## Logic Chain
1. Prior blockchains (Ethereum) had throughput and cost constraints incompatible with mass market usage.
2. Solana has overcome those constraints: 1B+ tx/day at <$0.01 per transaction.
3. This throughput and cost profile matches or exceeds traditional financial network volumes.
4. Therefore blockchain infrastructure has crossed the readiness threshold for internet-scale capital markets.
