# ARK Invest - Stablecoins now perform much of the emerging-market purchasing-power hedge role

## Position
The episode says stablecoins are increasingly the practical hedge against local currency erosion in many emerging markets.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript explicitly notes stablecoins are serving an insurance role against destruction of purchasing power.
- Speakers describe stablecoin usage in economies with currency stress as a bottom-up adoption path.
- Discussion contrasts this with earlier expectations that Bitcoin would dominate that specific use case.

## Logic Chain
1. Users under currency stress prioritize low-friction dollar exposure for transactions and savings.
2. Stablecoins offer near-immediate digital dollar access.
3. Therefore stablecoins can dominate the day-to-day hedge function in emerging markets.
