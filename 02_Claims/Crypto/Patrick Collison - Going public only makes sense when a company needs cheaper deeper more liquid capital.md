# Patrick Collison - Going public only makes sense when a company needs cheaper deeper more liquid capital

## Position
Patrick Collison says the real reason to go public is access to cheaper, deeper, more liquid capital, not prestige.

## Evidence Cited
- He says there is "no spiritual status" associated with being public.
- He says the point of being public is cheaper and more liquid capital.
- He says if a company wants that capital, then it should go public.

## Logic Chain
1. Patrick strips away status-based arguments for IPOs.
2. He replaces them with a capital-access rationale.
3. Therefore the claim captures his explicit framework for IPO timing.
