# Molly Bloom - She refused to cooperate against her players because their exposure came from her own bad decisions

## Position
Molly Bloom says she would not cooperate against the people in her games because she believed their legal exposure came downstream of her own choices and she did not want to save herself that way.

## Evidence Cited
- She says prosecutors offered to ease her outcome if she worked with them against players.
- She says she viewed the legal situation as the product of her own decisions.
- She says turning around and dragging those people through that process was not something she wanted to live with.

## Logic Chain

2. She accepts responsibility for creating the environment that exposed others.
3. That supports the claim that she refused cooperation on accountability grounds.
