# Tucker Carlson - Western decline is fundamentally a population and cultural collapse rather than just a policy mistake

## Position
Tucker Carlson argues that Western decline is fundamentally a population and cultural collapse, not merely a series of ordinary policy errors.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the real story in Europe and the broader West is population change and the cultural consequences that follow from it.
- He ties declining fertility, secular pessimism, and assisted-suicide politics to a deeper civilizational despair.
- He treats those trends as evidence of societies that no longer want to continue themselves.

## Logic Chain
1. Carlson sees low fertility and demographic self-replacement as symptoms of a cultural death drive.
2. He argues that policy debates are downstream of that broader loss of confidence in life and continuity.
3. That supports the claim that Western decline is fundamentally a population and cultural collapse.
