# Tulsi Gabbard - Classified power has to be brought back under normal democratic constraints if elite institutions are going to be legitimate

## Position
Tulsi Gabbard argues that intelligence institutions cannot remain legitimate if classified powers drift outside normal democratic oversight and accountability.

## Evidence Cited
- The interview centers on whether classified power can be brought back under normal democratic constraints.
- She links the issue to a broader legitimacy crisis in elite institutions.
- She treats the release of files as part of reasserting accountable governance.

## Logic Chain
1. Gabbard is connecting intelligence secrecy to democratic legitimacy rather than just to efficiency.
2. She sees oversight and constraint as prerequisites for institutional trust.
3. That supports the claim that classified power must be pulled back under democratic limits.
