# Maria Corina Machado - Technology let the opposition bypass censorship organize at scale and prove the vote

## Position
Maria Corina Machado says technology was essential because it let the Venezuelan opposition organize outside regime-controlled media and document the real election result.

## Evidence Cited
- She says she would not have won earlier races without the internet because traditional media would not give her access.
- She says the opposition later used apps, connectivity, and distributed processing centers to monitor polling stations and collect tally sheets.
- She says technology made it possible to publish the underlying evidence of victory for the world to inspect.

## Logic Chain
1. Machado treats technology as both an organizational tool and a verification tool.
2. She says it helped bypass censorship while also preserving auditable records of the vote.
3. That supports the claim that technology was crucial to organizing and proving the result.
