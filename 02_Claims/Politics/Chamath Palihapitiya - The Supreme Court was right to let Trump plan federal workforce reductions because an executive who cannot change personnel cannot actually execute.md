# Chamath Palihapitiya - The Supreme Court was right to let Trump plan federal workforce reductions because an executive who cannot change personnel cannot actually execute

## Position
Chamath Palihapitiya argues that the Supreme Court was right to let Trump plan federal workforce reductions because an executive who cannot change personnel cannot actually execute.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the president should have leeway to decide how the people reporting to him do their jobs.
- He compares blocking personnel changes to letting a CEO run a company while forbidding changes to the team.
- He says limiting workforce changes only compounds slow processes, downstream spending, and rule growth.

## Logic Chain
1. Palihapitiya ties executive accountability directly to control over staffing.
2. If the executive is responsible for outcomes but cannot change personnel, the role becomes functionally incoherent.
3. That supports the claim that planning workforce reductions is part of real executive authority.
