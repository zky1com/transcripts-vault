# Ron Johnson - Republicans cannot campaign on fiscal repair and then ignore arithmetic once they are governing

## Position
Ron Johnson says Republicans lose coherence if they run on fiscal repair and then abandon straightforward arithmetic as soon as they control the legislative process.

## Evidence Cited
- He argues Republicans cannot campaign on repair and then pass packages that fail their own deficit test.
- He criticizes Republican spending habits as well as Democratic ones.
- He frames the fight in arithmetic terms rather than loyalty terms.

## Logic Chain
1. Johnson is attacking inconsistency inside his own coalition.
2. He treats arithmetic as the core governing test.
3. That supports the claim that Republicans cannot credibly ignore the math once in power.
