# Chamath Palihapitiya - A new Musk-backed political party would need candidates with mass-distribution and communication skill more than a detailed shared agenda

## Position
Chamath Palihapitiya argues that a new Musk-backed political party would need candidates with mass distribution and communication skill more than a tightly shared agenda.

## Evidence Cited
- He says people should vote on names with built-in recognition rather than on a shared platform.
- He compares the model to Schwarzenegger running on enormous name recognition.
- He says modern candidates must be able to talk for hours on podcasts because that is the new political medium.

## Logic Chain
1. Palihapitiya treats distribution and communication as the scarcest assets in modern politics.
2. He argues a third party gains traction by recruiting already-recognized figures who can hold audience attention.
3. That supports the claim that such a party needs mass-distribution candidates more than a detailed common agenda.
