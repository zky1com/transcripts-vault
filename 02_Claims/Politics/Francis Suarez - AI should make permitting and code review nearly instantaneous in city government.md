# Francis Suarez - AI should make permitting and code review nearly instantaneous in city government

## Position
Francis Suarez argues that AI should reduce most permitting and code-review work to near-instant automated decisions.

## Evidence Cited
- He says most zoning and code decisions are repetitive applications of stable rules.
- He argues that computers should be able to check submissions, flag required changes, and return decisions immediately.
- He says the current six-to-eighteen-month permitting timelines are unnecessary with technology already available today.

## Logic Chain
1. Suarez treats the permitting backlog as a workflow problem more than a legal impossibility.
2. He believes the rules are structured enough for automated review in most cases.
3. That supports the claim that AI should make most city permitting close to instantaneous.
