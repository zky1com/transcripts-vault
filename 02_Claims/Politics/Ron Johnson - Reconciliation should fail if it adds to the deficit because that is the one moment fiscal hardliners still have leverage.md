# Ron Johnson - Reconciliation should fail if it adds to the deficit because that is the one moment fiscal hardliners still have leverage

## Position
Ron Johnson says reconciliation should not pass if it adds to the deficit and argues that this legislative moment is one of the few times fiscal hardliners still have meaningful leverage.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the first goal should be do not add to the deficit.
- He calls the bill fight the one opportunity to use leverage.
- He says he cannot be pressured into support.

## Logic Chain
1. Johnson is tying the deficit test directly to the leverage available in reconciliation.
2. He implies fiscal hawks lose credibility if they surrender here.
3. That supports the claim that reconciliation should fail if it adds to the deficit.
