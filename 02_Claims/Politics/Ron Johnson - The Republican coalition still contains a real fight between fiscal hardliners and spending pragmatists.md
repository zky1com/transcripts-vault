# Ron Johnson - The Republican coalition still contains a real fight between fiscal hardliners and spending pragmatists

## Position

## Evidence Cited
- His criticism targets Republican spending habits as much as Democratic ones.
- He describes the bill fight as a moment of leverage inside the Senate.
- He frames the episode as exposing tension between discipline and pragmatism.

## Logic Chain

2. He thinks the coalition still has a live internal fiscal conflict.
3. That supports the claim that a real hardliner-versus-pragmatist fight remains.
