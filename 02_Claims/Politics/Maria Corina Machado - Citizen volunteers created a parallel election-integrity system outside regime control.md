# Maria Corina Machado - Citizen volunteers created a parallel election-integrity system outside regime control

## Position
Maria Corina Machado says citizen volunteers built a parallel election-integrity system outside regime control to monitor polling places and publish the real results.

## Evidence Cited
- She says the opposition trained hundreds of thousands of people online and eventually mobilized more than one million volunteers with specific roles.
- She says they created operational centers, smuggled in connectivity equipment, and geospatially organized the processing of tally sheets.
- She says they published digitized original poll sheets that were later audited by independent experts.

## Logic Chain
1. Machado presents election defense as a distributed citizen operation, not something outsourced to institutions the regime controlled.
2. She says the apparatus was designed both to monitor voting and to preserve the proof afterward.
3. That supports the claim that volunteers built a parallel integrity system.
