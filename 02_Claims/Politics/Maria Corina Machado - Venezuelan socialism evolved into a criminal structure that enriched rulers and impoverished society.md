# Maria Corina Machado - Venezuelan socialism evolved into a criminal structure that enriched rulers and impoverished society

## Position
Maria Corina Machado says Venezuela's socialist regime evolved from populism into a criminal structure that concentrated wealth and power at the top while impoverishing the country.

## Evidence Cited
- She says the regime became a real criminal structure over 26 years.
- She describes massive corruption, loyalty-buying, and elite fortunes alongside pensions below one dollar a month.
- She says the regime moved beyond ideology into drug trafficking, gold smuggling, arms smuggling, and human trafficking.

## Logic Chain
1. Machado portrays the regime as predatory rather than merely misguided.
2. She links socialist control of the economy to corruption, repression, and criminal revenue streams.
3. That supports the claim that the system enriched rulers while degrading society.
