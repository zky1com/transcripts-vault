# Antonio Gracias - The same noncitizen Social Security pipeline now connects to benefits access and probable illegal voting exposure

## Position
Antonio Gracias argues that the noncitizen Social Security pipeline now connects not only to benefits access but also to probable illegal voting exposure.

## Evidence Cited
- He says they found 1.3 million of the identified group currently on Medicaid.
- He says every benefit program they looked at was being accessed by these people.
- He says they searched voter rolls in friendly states and found thousands registered to vote, with many having actually voted.

## Logic Chain
1. Gracias links the issuance pipeline to downstream eligibility and participation systems.
2. He presents voting-roll matches as evidence that the risk extended beyond administrative benefits.
3. That supports the claim that this pipeline now connects to benefits access and probable illegal voting exposure.
