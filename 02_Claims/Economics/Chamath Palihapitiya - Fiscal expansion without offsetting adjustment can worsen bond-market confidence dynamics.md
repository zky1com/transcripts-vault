# Chamath Palihapitiya - Fiscal expansion without offsetting adjustment can worsen bond-market confidence dynamics

## Position
Large new debt additions without credible offsets can pressure investor confidence in long-duration sovereign paper.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript discusses concern about adding debt while rates are already elevated.
- Panel connects fiscal path credibility to bond-market response.
- Discussion frames confidence as endogenous to policy mix.

## Logic Chain
1. Bond investors price both level and trajectory of debt.
2. Weak trajectory confidence raises required yields.
3. Higher yields further stress fiscal math and confidence.
