# Stephanie Drews - High accountability cultures are reinforced by tools not solutions and one step forward daily discipline

## Position
Stephanie argues that developing tools for autonomy and maintaining daily forward motion are core mechanisms for overcoming setbacks.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says she aimed to give children tools rather than complete solutions.
- She emphasizes confronting mistakes directly and continuing with at least one forward step each day.
- Discussion frames this approach as transferable from family context to organizational leadership.

## Logic Chain
1. Tool-based development builds independent problem-solving capacity.
2. Daily progress discipline preserves momentum through setbacks.
3. Together these practices create an accountability-oriented execution culture.
