# Cathie Wood - Disruptive innovation stocks are set for 40-50% compound annual returns over the next five years

## Position
After four years of capital starvation during which the MAG 6 tripled while disruptive innovation gained only 30%, the rubber band has snapped back — ARK projects roughly 40-50% compound annual returns in truly disruptive innovation over the next five years.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "The truly disruptive innovation we would expect... during the next five years to deliver a compound annual rate of return of roughly 50%. Now we've had some of that so maybe it's 40 to 45% compound annual rate of change."
- Transcript: "From 2019 to 2024, the MAG 6 tripled in valuation/market cap. Whereas truly disruptive innovation... went up only 30%."
- Transcript: "A rubber band has been stretching for the last four years and it let go with the election of Donald Trump. That's when truly disruptive innovation started to shine."
- Transcript: Trump's tax package — full depreciation of structures, equipment, R&D, software in year 1 — described as a major catalyst accelerating the repricing.

## Logic Chain
1. Capital concentrated in mega-cap safe names from 2019-2024, creating a valuation divergence from disruptive innovation.
2. Policy tailwinds (Trump tax cuts, deregulation) and new investment time horizons are broadening market participation.
3. The valuation gap relative to expected fundamental outcomes creates a mathematical return opportunity.
4. Therefore compound returns significantly above the broad market are projected for the next 5-year cycle.
