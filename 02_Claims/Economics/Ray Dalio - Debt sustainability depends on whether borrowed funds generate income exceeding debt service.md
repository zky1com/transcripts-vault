# 
Ray Dalio - Debt sustainability depends on whether borrowed funds generate income exceeding debt service

## Position
Debt remains healthy when financed activity produces income that exceeds servicing costs; otherwise debt accumulation becomes self-reinforcing and destabilizing.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says the key test is whether debt creates income more than enough to service debt.
- Dalio describes debt burdens as constricting economic circulation when service costs consume spending capacity.
- He frames this dynamic as observable and measurable, not just opinion.

## Logic Chain
1. Borrowing is sustainable only if returns exceed financing obligations.
2. When service costs outpace income generation, refinancing needs compound.
3. Therefore debt quality is determined by cash-flow productivity, not debt size alone.
