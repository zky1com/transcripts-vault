# Cathie Wood - AI-led productivity gains can raise real growth while easing inflation pressure

## Position
The episode argues productivity acceleration from AI can lift real GDP and simultaneously push inflation lower.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says productivity is expected to be a major upside surprise in the recovery.
- Cathie states higher real growth has historically coincided with higher productivity and lower inflation.
- AI is described as strongly deflationary through cost declines and scaling unit output.

## Logic Chain
1. Productivity growth raises output per unit of input.
2. Higher productivity can absorb wage and demand pressures without equivalent price increases.
3. So AI-driven productivity can support growth while disinflating.
