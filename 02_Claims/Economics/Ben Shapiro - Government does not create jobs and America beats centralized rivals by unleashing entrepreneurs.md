# Ben Shapiro - Government does not create jobs and America beats centralized rivals by unleashing entrepreneurs

## Position
Ben Shapiro argues that government does not create jobs and that America beats centralized rivals by unleashing entrepreneurs.

## Evidence Cited
- He says politicians are wrong when they claim they created jobs.
- He says government can only take money from some people and give it to others, while entrepreneurs are the ones who actually create jobs.
- He says the way to beat China is by unleashing the collective knowledge, wisdom, and ambition of the American people rather than relying on centralized direction.

## Logic Chain
1. Shapiro distinguishes resource redistribution from genuine job creation.
2. He ties American competitiveness to decentralized entrepreneurial action rather than state planning.
3. That supports the claim that government does not create jobs and America wins by unleashing entrepreneurs.
