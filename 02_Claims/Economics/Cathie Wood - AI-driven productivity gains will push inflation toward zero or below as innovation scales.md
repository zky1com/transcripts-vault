# Cathie Wood - AI-driven productivity gains will push inflation toward zero or below as innovation scales

## Position
As five innovation platforms converge and drive productivity at unprecedented scale, ARK expects inflation to surprise significantly to the downside — potentially reaching 0% or negative as the disinflationary force of technology overwhelms monetary expansion.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "We think that inflation is going to surprise significantly on the low side of expectations. We would not be surprised to see 0% inflation or less as we exit the tariffs here."
- Transcript: The five innovation platforms and their convergence are cited as the mechanism — productivity gains across every sector simultaneously deflate costs.
- Transcript: Trump's tax package (full first-year depreciation on structures, equipment, R&D, software) is described as likely to "turbocharge" the investment accelerating these deflationary trends.

## Logic Chain
1. Five technology platforms are improving cost curves across energy, manufacturing, healthcare, and software simultaneously.
2. Technology-driven cost deflation is structural and compounds over time.
3. As tariff pressures ease and innovation accelerates, the deflationary impulse overwhelms monetary inflation.
4. Therefore 0% or negative CPI is a credible near-term scenario.
