# Howard Lutnick - America can reach 5 to 6 percent GDP growth if investment rates and domestic buildout line up

## Position
Howard Lutnick says the United States can reach 5 to 6 percent GDP growth if tariffs, domestic factory buildout, and lower rates reinforce each other.

## Evidence Cited
- He predicts the economy can move into the fives and even the sixes if rates fall.
- He ties that outcome to visible factory construction, industrial expansion, and stronger investment.
- He presents the current moment as a once-in-a-generation domestic build cycle.

## Logic Chain
1. Lutnick sees capital formation and industrial construction as the main drivers of faster growth.
2. He says rates and policy can amplify that cycle rather than choke it off.
3. That supports the claim that the US can reach 5 to 6 percent growth if those levers line up.
