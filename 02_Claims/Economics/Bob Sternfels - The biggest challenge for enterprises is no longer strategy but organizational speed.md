# Bob Sternfels - The biggest challenge for enterprises is no longer strategy but organizational speed

## Position
Bob Sternfels says the biggest challenge for enterprises is increasingly organizational speed rather than high-level strategy.

## Evidence Cited
- He says nearly every CEO he meets is asking how to make the organization move faster.
- He describes warp-speed technological change and pressure on companies across verticals.
- He says the issue is often less deciding what to do than getting the enterprise to actually adapt fast enough.

## Logic Chain
1. Sternfels argues that rapid technological change compresses the time available for execution.
2. That makes organizational responsiveness more important than static planning alone.
3. That supports the claim that enterprises now struggle more with speed than with strategy.
