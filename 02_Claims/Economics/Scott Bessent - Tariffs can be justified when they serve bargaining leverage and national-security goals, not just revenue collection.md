# Scott Bessent - Tariffs can be justified when they serve bargaining leverage and national-security goals, not just revenue collection

## Position
Scott Bessent argues that tariffs can be legitimate policy tools when they support bargaining leverage and national-security goals rather than being viewed only as blunt tax instruments.

## Evidence Cited
- He frames tariffs as tools that can serve bargaining and national-security goals.
- He defends tariff strategy as part of a broader policy framework.
- He does not describe tariffs as a one-dimensional revenue tool.

## Logic Chain
1. Bessent is making a strategic rather than purely budgetary case for tariffs.
2. He links them to leverage and national resilience.
3. That supports the claim that he sees tariffs as justified when they serve those broader goals.
