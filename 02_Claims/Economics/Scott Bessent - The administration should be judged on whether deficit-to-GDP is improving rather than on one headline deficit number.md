# Scott Bessent - The administration should be judged on whether deficit-to-GDP is improving rather than on one headline deficit number

## Position
Scott Bessent says the right fiscal test is whether the deficit path relative to GDP is improving over time, not whether one isolated headline number looks clean on a given day.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the administration should be judged on the direction of deficit to GDP.
- He frames 2025 as a setup year rather than the whole verdict.
- He repeatedly ties credibility to a believable medium-term path.

## Logic Chain
1. Bessent is pushing a directional and medium-term fiscal metric.
2. He is resisting snap judgments off single headline prints.
3. That supports the claim that deficit-to-GDP direction is his preferred scorecard.
