# Hemant Taneja - The new venture playbook is to combine startups with existing customer bases so AI transformation happens faster

## Position
Hemant Taneja argues that a new venture playbook is emerging in which investors pair startups with existing customer bases and operating assets so AI transformation can happen much faster.

## Evidence Cited
- He describes acquiring incumbent assets like health systems or service businesses to give founders real customer access.
- He says the point is not traditional private equity optimization but faster deployment of AI into existing operations.
- He frames this as a way to compress value creation and distribution for startups.

## Logic Chain
1. Taneja believes distribution and operating context are major bottlenecks for AI startups.
2. He argues that attaching startups to incumbent customer bases removes that bottleneck.
3. That supports the claim that the new playbook pairs startups with existing assets so AI transformation happens faster.
