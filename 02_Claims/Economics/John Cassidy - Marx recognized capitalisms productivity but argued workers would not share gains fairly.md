# John Cassidy - Marx recognized capitalisms productivity but argued workers would not share gains fairly

## Position
John Cassidy says Marx and Engels viewed capitalism as highly innovative and expansionary but believed its distributional logic systematically favored capital over labor.

## Evidence Cited
