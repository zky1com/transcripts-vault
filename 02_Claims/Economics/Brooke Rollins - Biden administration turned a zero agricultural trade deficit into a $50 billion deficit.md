# Brooke Rollins - Biden administration turned a zero agricultural trade deficit into a $50 billion deficit

## Position
The US agricultural trade balance went from zero deficit under Trump's first term to a $50 billion deficit under Biden — representing $50 billion effectively extracted from American farmers' pockets through unfavorable trade conditions.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "Under Joe Biden we lost $50 billion in trade. We went from a zero trade deficit with Trump one to a $50 billion trade deficit with Biden."
- Transcript: "That's $50 billion almost directly out of our farmers' pockets."
- Transcript: Rollins presenting this as a core agricultural policy failure requiring correction under Trump's second term.

## Logic Chain
1. Agricultural trade balance reflects the competitive position of US farmers vs. foreign producers.
2. A zero deficit baseline shows American agriculture was competitive under prior trade policy.
3. A $50B swing to deficit under Biden indicates policy changes disadvantaged US agricultural exports or advantaged imports.
4. Therefore the Biden agricultural trade record directly harmed American farm income.
