# Nigel Coe - US power-demand growth has accelerated enough to require major new generation and grid investment

## Position
Nigel Coe contends that US electricity demand has shifted from a long low-growth phase to materially higher growth, forcing large additions in generation, transmission, and distribution capacity.

## Evidence Cited
