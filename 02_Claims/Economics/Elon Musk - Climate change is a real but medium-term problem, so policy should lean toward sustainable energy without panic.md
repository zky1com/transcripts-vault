# Elon Musk - Climate change is a real but medium-term problem, so policy should lean toward sustainable energy without panic

## Position
Elon Musk argues that climate change is real but medium-term, so policy should lean toward sustainable energy without panic.

## Evidence Cited
- He says carbon concentration in the atmosphere can be measured and is steadily increasing.
- He says the right order of magnitude is roughly 50 years before climate change becomes a serious issue, not 5 years and not 500.
- He says the reasonable course of action is to lean toward sustainable energy and remove distortions rather than rely on alarmism.

## Logic Chain
1. Musk rejects both denial and near-term apocalypse as poor descriptions of the problem.
2. He frames the policy response as directional but measured: move toward sustainable energy without hysteria.
3. That supports the claim that climate change is a real but medium-term problem requiring a steady energy transition.
