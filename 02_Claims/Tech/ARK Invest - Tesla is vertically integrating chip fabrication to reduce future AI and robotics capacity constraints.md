# ARK Invest - Tesla is vertically integrating chip fabrication to reduce future AI and robotics capacity constraints

## Position
The panel says Tesla intends to build its own chip fabrication capacity because external supply is expected to be a binding constraint for vehicles and Optimus.

- Transcript states Tesla announced plans to build its own chip fab.
- Speakers cite concerns that chip availability from existing foundries may be insufficient.
- Discussion links this directly to expected scale requirements for both vehicles and humanoid robots.

## Logic Chain

2. AI and robotics roadmaps are increasingly chip limited.
3. So in house fabrication is framed as a supply security move.
