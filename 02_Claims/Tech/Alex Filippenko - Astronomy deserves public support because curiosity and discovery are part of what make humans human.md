# Alex Filippenko - Astronomy deserves public support because curiosity and discovery are part of what make humans human

## Position
Alex Filippenko argues that basic astronomy deserves public funding because the pursuit of deep questions and discovery is part of what makes humans distinct.

## Evidence Cited
- He says humans uniquely ask abstract questions about origins and build instruments to pursue answers.
- He argues that abandoning this pursuit would sell humanity short as homo sapiens.
- He also says astronomy acts as a gateway science that motivates people into STEM careers.

## Logic Chain
1. Filippenko grounds the case for astronomy in both human meaning and downstream educational benefit.
2. He argues that pure research is not wasteful even when its benefits are not immediately commercial.
3. That supports the claim that astronomy deserves public support as an expression of human curiosity and discovery.
