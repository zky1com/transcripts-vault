# Cleo Abram - Many viewers want ambitious science coverage that explains progress without defaulting to doom

## Position
Cleo Abram argues that a large audience wants science and technology coverage that is ambitious and understandable without collapsing into fear-based framing.

## Evidence Cited
- She says there is a lot of lucrative fear-mongering in media.
- She says she wanted to build an optimistic show that helps people understand hard problems and imagine what could go right.
- She says YouTube revealed there were millions of viewers looking for that type of work.

## Logic Chain
1. Abram contrasts fear-driven media incentives with her own audience response.
2. She presents audience growth as proof that optimism and technical clarity are not niche preferences.
3. That supports the claim that many viewers want ambitious progress-focused science coverage.
