# Travis Kalanick - If autonomous driving works it can unlock both robotaxis and automated food logistics because the same autonomy stack moves people or things

## Position
Travis Kalanick argues that a working autonomy stack can unlock both robotaxis and automated food logistics because the same core system can move people or things.

## Evidence Cited
- He says if you get the autonomy problem right, you can apply it to both moving food and moving people.
- He links his interest in Pony-style autonomy to both mobility and food delivery.
- He says there are many parties interested in autonomy because one solution can serve multiple markets.

## Logic Chain
1. Kalanick frames autonomy as a general transportation capability, not a single-vertical product.
2. He argues that once the driving problem is solved, the same stack can be repurposed across use cases.
3. That supports the claim that one autonomy stack can unlock both robotaxis and automated food logistics.
