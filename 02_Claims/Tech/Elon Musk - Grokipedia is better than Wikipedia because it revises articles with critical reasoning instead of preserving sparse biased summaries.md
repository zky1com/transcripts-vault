# Elon Musk - Grokipedia is better than Wikipedia because it revises articles with critical reasoning instead of preserving sparse biased summaries

## Position
Elon Musk argues that Grokipedia is better than Wikipedia because it revises articles with critical reasoning instead of preserving sparse and biased summaries.

## Evidence Cited
- He says they trained a maximally truth-seeking version of Grok that breaks arguments into axioms and tests coherence.
- He says that model cycled through the million most popular Wikipedia articles to add, modify, and delete based on broader public information.
- He says Wikipedia is sparse, wrong, out of date, and often biased, while Grokipedia is more comprehensive and accurate.

## Logic Chain
1. Musk grounds the comparison in the method used to revise articles, not just in branding.
2. He argues that critical reasoning plus broader synthesis produces fuller and more neutral entries.
3. That supports the claim that Grokipedia is superior because it revises articles with critical reasoning.
