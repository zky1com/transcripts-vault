# Neal Mohan - YouTube should label AI-generated content and build likeness controls rather than ban AI content by default

## Position
Neal Mohan says YouTube should handle AI media with transparency labels and likeness controls instead of assuming AI-generated content is inherently violative.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the distinction between AI-generated and AI-assisted content will increasingly be a continuum.
- He says YouTube uses labels to disclose AI-generated content.
- He says the deeper issue is creator likeness and describes building likeness detection so creators can control unauthorized uses.

## Logic Chain
1. Neal rejects a blanket ban approach to AI-made media.
2. He favors disclosure and rights-management tools instead.
3. Therefore the claim captures his policy approach to AI content on YouTube.
