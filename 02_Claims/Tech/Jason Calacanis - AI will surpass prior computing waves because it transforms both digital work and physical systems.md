# Jason Calacanis - AI will surpass prior computing waves because it transforms both digital work and physical systems

## Position
Jason Calacanis argues that AI will surpass prior computing waves because it does not stay inside software alone and instead transforms both digital work and physical systems.

## Evidence Cited
- He says AI will dwarf the PC, internet, cloud, and mobile revolutions.
- He frames CES categories like self-driving, robotics, manufacturing, and AR as downstream expressions of AI.
- He describes AI as the most important transformation of their lifetimes.

## Logic Chain
1. Calacanis treats AI as a general capability layer rather than one isolated product cycle.
2. He says its impact is larger because it changes physical labor and industrial systems as well as software.
3. That supports the claim that AI will surpass prior computing waves by transforming both digital and physical systems.
