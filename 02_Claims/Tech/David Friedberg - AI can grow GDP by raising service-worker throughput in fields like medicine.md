# David Friedberg - AI can grow GDP by raising service-worker throughput in fields like medicine

## Position
David Friedberg says AI can expand GDP by increasing the throughput of service providers such as doctors.

## Evidence Cited
- He says lower diagnosis costs and AI leverage would let doctors see many more patients per day.
- He says more preventative care becomes available when throughput rises.
- He says that dynamic would grow the size of the relevant economic sector.

## Logic Chain
1. Friedberg links AI directly to service capacity expansion.
2. He argues more capacity means more billable output and more access.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view that AI productivity can translate into GDP growth.
