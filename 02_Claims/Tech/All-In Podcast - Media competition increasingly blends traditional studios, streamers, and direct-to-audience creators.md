# All-In Podcast - Media competition increasingly blends traditional studios, streamers, and direct-to-audience creators

## Position
The competitive set is no longer studio-vs-studio but a mixed ecosystem where creator brands compete directly with incumbents.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript references creators like MrBeast alongside major platforms and studios.
- Panel repeatedly describes the environment as chaotic due to overlapping models.
- Conversation positions direct creator distribution as durable competitive force.

## Logic Chain
1. Lower distribution friction enables creator scale.
2. Creator brands can command audience loyalty independent of studios.
3. Therefore incumbent media must compete in a structurally expanded field.
