# Neal Mohan - YouTube's creator economy works because creators bet on themselves and scale with audience growth

## Position
Neal Mohan says YouTube's creator economy is fundamentally entrepreneurial because creators bet on themselves and their monetization rises with their audience growth.

## Evidence Cited
- He says YouTube is a creator economy in the true entrepreneurial sense.
- He says creators are betting on themselves rather than following the traditional media model.
- He says as audience success grows, the creator's monetization and business also grow.

## Logic Chain
1. Neal contrasts YouTube's model with upfront traditional media economics.
2. He ties creator upside directly to audience growth.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view of YouTube as an entrepreneurial creator platform.
