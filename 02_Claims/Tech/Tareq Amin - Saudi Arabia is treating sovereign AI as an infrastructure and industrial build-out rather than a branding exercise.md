# Tareq Amin - Saudi Arabia is treating sovereign AI as an infrastructure and industrial build-out rather than a branding exercise

## Position
Tareq Amin says Saudi Arabia's AI strategy is fundamentally about building the underlying infrastructure, talent, and operating systems required for national capability, not just talking about frontier models.

## Evidence Cited
- He says sovereign AI is an industrial strategy.
- He describes the build-out in terms of infrastructure, talent, and execution.
- He says capital alone is not enough and those components must move together.

## Logic Chain
1. Amin frames national AI capability as a systems build problem.
2. He repeatedly emphasizes physical and organizational infrastructure over slogan-level ambition.
3. That supports the claim that the Saudi approach is industrial and infrastructural rather than cosmetic.
