# Elon Musk - X will become far more useful once Grok reads and classifies the platform semantically instead of relying on legacy heuristics

## Position
Elon Musk argues that X will become far more useful once Grok reads and classifies posts semantically instead of relying on legacy recommendation heuristics.

## Evidence Cited
- He says Grok will start by reading what it thinks are the top 10 million of the 100 million daily posts and then expand to the full set.
- He says Grok will understand, categorize, and match those posts to users.
- He says semantic search will let users ask plain-language questions and retrieve matching text, pictures, and video.

## Logic Chain
1. Musk describes a shift from heuristic ranking toward full semantic understanding of platform content.
2. He presents both feed ranking and search as downstream products of that semantic layer.
3. That supports the claim that X becomes more useful once Grok reads and classifies the platform semantically.
