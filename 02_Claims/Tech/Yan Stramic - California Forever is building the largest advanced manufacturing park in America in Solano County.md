# Yan Stramic - California Forever is building the largest advanced manufacturing park in America in Solano County

## Position
California Forever has raised over $1 billion and acquired ~70,000 acres in Solano County to build the Solano Foundry — set to be the largest advanced manufacturing park in America — based on the thesis that competing with China requires factories of the future powered by robotics and AI, not low-wage labor.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "We're going to start by building the Solano Foundry which will be the largest advanced manufacturing park in America."
- Transcript: "Over the decade we've raised over a billion dollars and acquired nearly 70,000 acres."
- Transcript: "We cannot compete with China by throwing bodies — we need factories of the future using robotics and AI."

## Logic Chain
1. US manufacturing has been hollowed out relative to China's scale and capacity.
2. Labor-cost competition with China is unwinnable at current US wage levels.
3. Robotics and AI enable capital-intensive, high-productivity manufacturing that competes on quality and speed rather than labor cost.
4. Therefore large-scale automated manufacturing investment on US soil is the viable path to industrial recompetitiveness.
