# Heath Raboy - The bitter lesson keeps winning because scale and compute still beat handcrafted AI tricks

## Position

- He says the bitter lesson keeps coming back.
- He treats Grok 4 as another datapoint showing that scale is still winning.
- He frames the current model race around broad capability gains from bigger systems rather than around narrow handcrafted tricks.

## Logic Chain
