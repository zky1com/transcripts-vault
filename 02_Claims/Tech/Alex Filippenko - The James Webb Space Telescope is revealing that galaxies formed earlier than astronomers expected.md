# Alex Filippenko - The James Webb Space Telescope is revealing that galaxies formed earlier than astronomers expected

## Position
Alex Filippenko says the James Webb Space Telescope is showing that galaxies started forming and evolving earlier than astronomers expected.

## Evidence Cited
- He describes Webb as seeing galaxies only a few hundred million years after the big bang.
- He says one of the surprising results is that galaxies appear to have started forming and evolving earlier than expected.
- He frames that as an active cosmological puzzle researchers are now working on.

## Logic Chain
1. Filippenko points to direct Webb observations as the source of the surprise.
2. He treats the timing mismatch as a substantive challenge to previous expectations.
3. That supports the claim that Webb is revealing earlier-than-expected galaxy formation.
