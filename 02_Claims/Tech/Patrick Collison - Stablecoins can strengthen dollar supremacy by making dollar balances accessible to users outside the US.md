# Patrick Collison - Stablecoins can strengthen dollar supremacy by making dollar balances accessible to users outside the US

## Position
Patrick Collison says stablecoins can reinforce the dollar's global role by giving non-US users direct access to dollar balances.

## Evidence Cited
- He says many people outside the US are subject to weaker local currencies.
- He says stablecoins let users in places like Ecuador access small dollar balances that were previously inaccessible.
- He says this could deepen the dollar's status as the world's reserve currency.

## Logic Chain
1. Patrick ties stablecoin adoption to real international demand for dollar exposure.
2. He then connects that demand to the dollar's global role.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view that stablecoins can extend dollar supremacy.
