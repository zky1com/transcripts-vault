# Tareq Amin - The biggest bottleneck in enterprise AI adoption is organizational mindset and culture rather than the models alone

## Position
Tareq Amin says the hard part of AI inside large enterprises is not merely model access but changing organizational mentality, culture, and workflows so people actually use the new system.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the biggest challenge had nothing to do with the technology.
- He says mindset, mentality, culture, and organization are underestimated constraints.
- He describes his company's work as changing how the enterprise operating system functions.

## Logic Chain
1. Amin is explicit that model capability is not enough to force enterprise transformation.
2. He identifies organizational change as the bigger implementation problem.
3. That supports the claim that culture and mindset are the true bottlenecks.
