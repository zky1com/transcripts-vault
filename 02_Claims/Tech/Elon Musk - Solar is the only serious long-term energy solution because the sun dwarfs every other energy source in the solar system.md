# Elon Musk - Solar is the only serious long-term energy solution because the sun dwarfs every other energy source in the solar system

## Position
Elon Musk argues that solar is the only serious long-term energy solution because the sun dwarfs every other energy source in the solar system.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the sun is about 99.8 percent of the mass of the solar system and that total energy production rounds to 100 percent from the sun.
- He says even adding or burning other planetary masses would not materially change that comparison.
- He says Earth can be fully powered with solar panels and batteries and that Tesla has already published the math showing there is no materials shortage for doing so.

## Logic Chain
1. Musk treats the sun's scale advantage as so overwhelming that all other energy options are secondary over the long run.
2. He pairs that physical argument with an engineering claim that Earth-scale solar-plus-storage is materially feasible.
3. That supports the claim that solar is the only serious long-term energy solution.
