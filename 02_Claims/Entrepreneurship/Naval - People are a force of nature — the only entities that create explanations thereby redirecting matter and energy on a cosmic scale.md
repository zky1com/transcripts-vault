# Naval - People are a force of nature — the only entities that create explanations thereby redirecting matter and energy on a cosmic scale

## Position

People are not passive observers of a universe that would continue without them. They are active agents that create explanations — causal models of what raw materials can be transformed into. Through those explanations, uranium goes from inert rock to nuclear fuel; silicon goes from sand to semiconductor. Without explanatory knowledge, these materials do nothing. This is what it means for people to be a force of nature: they are the entities whose knowledge redirects the future trajectory of matter and energy. No other physical process does this. Gravity, thermodynamics, electromagnetism — these follow fixed laws and cannot choose new outcomes. People, by creating new knowledge, can choose outcomes that were previously impossible and direct physical processes toward those outcomes. This gives people a causal reach that no other known physical system possesses.

## Logic Chain

1. Raw materials have no inherent use value — uranium is just rock without knowledge of nuclear physics
2. Explanatory knowledge reveals what raw materials can be transformed into
3. Once transformed, they redirect physical processes toward outcomes that would not otherwise occur
4. Gravity and other physical forces follow deterministic laws — they cannot choose new outcomes
5. People, via knowledge creation, can direct physical systems toward any outcome not ruled out by physics
6. Therefore: people are a force of nature — not by brute force but by knowledge-directed physical causation
