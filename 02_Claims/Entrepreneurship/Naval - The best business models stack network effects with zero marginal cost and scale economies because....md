# Naval - The best business models stack network effects with zero marginal cost and scale economies because...

## Position

Scale economies (cheaper per unit at volume), zero marginal cost (adding a user costs nothing), and network effects (each user adds value for others) tend to co-occur in the best technology businesses. Zero marginal cost products naturally have scale economies; zero marginal cost distribution enables network effect hooks. Deliberately picking a business model that stacks all three is the most powerful leverage decision a founder can make.

- "Customers adding value to each other while you sleep" as the ideal ultimate leverage state

## Logic Chain

1. Zero marginal cost: adding users costs nothing, revenue compounds, so margins improve infinitely with scale
2. Scale economies: production cost per unit falls with volume, creating competitive moats
3. Network effects: users actively make the product better for each other, increasing retention
4. These three amplify each other: scale funds growth, zero cost enables network effects, network effects lock in scale
5. A business with all three becomes compounding and nearly unassailable
