# Naval - High-turnover environments like politics make it structurally harder to play long-term trust-based...

## Position

## Logic Chain

1. Long-term game-playing requires knowing the other person will be present for future rounds
2. High-turnover environments eliminate this assurance
3. Without assurance of future interaction, short-term defection becomes rational
4. Therefore industries with stable, long-term participants structurally produce more cooperation
5. The "corruption" of career politicians is the price of the cooperation they enable
