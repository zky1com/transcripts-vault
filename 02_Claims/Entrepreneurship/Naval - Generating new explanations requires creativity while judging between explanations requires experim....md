## Position

Generating new explanations is an act of creativity â€” it cannot be reduced to any algorithmic process, including Bayesian updating. New theories do not emerge from updating on data; they emerge from imaginative leaps that create something genuinely new. Bayesianism plays no role in either of these processes. It is a probability revision tool for finite hypothesis spaces. Science uses it in medicine and in constrained probability problems.
