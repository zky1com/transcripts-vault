# Naval - Real feedback comes only from free markets and nature — feedback from people is fake because groups...

## Position

Almost all feedback humans receive from other people is false. Awards are fake. Critical praise is fake. Family approval is fake. Friend endorsements are fake. Even when individuals try to be honest, the social context in which feedback is given is contaminated by the need to get along, avoid conflict, and maintain group cohesion. The result is that every feedback signal passing through people is filtered for social acceptability rather than accuracy. The only feedback mechanisms that reliably cut through this are free markets and physical nature. Markets are brutal: either people buy your product or they don't, regardless of how much they like you. Nature is harsher still: either your rocket launches or it explodes; either your drone flies within tolerance or it crashes. You cannot negotiate with nature. You cannot charm a free market into falsely validating a bad product. These are the only truth-telling mechanisms available to us because they have no social needs â€” they cannot be fooled.
