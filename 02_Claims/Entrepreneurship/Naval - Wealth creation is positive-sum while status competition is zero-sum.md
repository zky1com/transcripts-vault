# Naval - Wealth creation is positive-sum while status competition is zero-sum

## Position
Wealth is a positive-sum game: more houses built makes building more houses easier; creating new things grows the pie for everyone. Status is zero-sum: for one person to rise in rank another must fall. Historically, hunter-gatherers were status-based societies; agricultural and industrial economies are wealth-based. Status games make people combative because winning requires putting others down. Journalists attacking the wealthy are often playing status games disguised as ethics.
