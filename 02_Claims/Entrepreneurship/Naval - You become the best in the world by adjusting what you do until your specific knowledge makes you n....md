## Logic Chain

1. Being number one is required to capture outsized returns in any field
2. You cannot choose to be number one at an arbitrary domain against billions of competitors
3. But your unique combination of skills, interests, context is something no one else has
4. Adjusting the definition of "what you do" until it maps to that unique combination yields number-one status by construction
5. The winner is not whoever tries hardest at the generic goal, but whoever found the niche where they are naturally supreme
