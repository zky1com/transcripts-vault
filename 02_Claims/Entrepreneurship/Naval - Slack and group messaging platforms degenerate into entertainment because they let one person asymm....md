## Position

## Logic Chain

1. Low-friction broadcast communication reduces the personal cost of sending messages below the social cost of receiving them
2. This asymmetry means the equilibrium shifts toward over-communication (everyone broadcasts, no one synthesizes)
3. Over-communication degrades into noise, which becomes an entertainment/social channel
4. The cure is friction: requiring point-to-point communication forces people to think before they ask and limits total communication volume
5. A smaller communication surface keeps the team small and creative work protected
