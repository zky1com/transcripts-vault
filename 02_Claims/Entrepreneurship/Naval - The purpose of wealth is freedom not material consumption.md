# Naval - The purpose of wealth is freedom not material consumption

## Position
The real reason to accumulate wealth is to buy freedom from mandatory work, alarm clocks, commuting, and soulless employment. Material luxuries (fur coats, Ferraris, yachts, private jets) become boring fast. The intrinsic value of wealth is being a sovereign individual answerable to no one.
