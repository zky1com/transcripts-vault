# Naval - Frontier environments always attract scammers and weirdos but they are where innovation happens — S...

## Position

Frontier environments (the wild west, early internet, crypto) are characterized by absence of established rules, which attracts both scammers (who exploit the vacuum) and genuine innovators (who need the vacuum to experiment without permission). The scams and crime are a necessary tax on the innovation: you cannot have the freedom to innovate without also having the freedom to defraud. ## Logic Chain

1. Innovation requires freedom to try unconventional approaches without needing permission
2. Permission-granting systems (HR departments, regulators, established institutions) filter out extreme personalities and heterodox approaches
3. Frontier environments, lacking these filters, allow both scammers and genuine innovators to operate
4. As a frontier institutionalizes, it gains safety (fewer scams) but loses innovation (more filtering)
5. Therefore: the current frontier (wherever permission-less experimentation is still possible) is where the next generation of transformative innovation will emerge
