## Position

## Logic Chain

1. Software is winner-take-all: one product can serve a global audience, so users will always adopt the best available option
2. When tools lower the barrier, more people build, but competition for users intensifies â€” raising quality expectations
3. Specialists who remain at the frontier of tools produce better products than generalists who use those tools shallowly
4. Therefore: each wave of democratization raises the floor (more people can build something) while preserving the ceiling for specialists
5. Unlike ditches (where one is not better than another), software competition is winner-take-all and the specialist premium persists
