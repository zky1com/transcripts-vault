# Naval - Actively pursuing the first three types of luck eventually neutralizes bad luck by reversion to the...

## Position

By continuously taking action (stirring the pot), developing expertise (prepared mind), and building a distinctive reputation (destiny luck), you essentially run out of bad luck. Either the repeated action eventually hits something, or the outcomes revert to the mean and your actual talent comes into play. The implication: bad luck is often just insufficient action, and it can be ground down through persistence across all non-blind-luck types.

## Logic Chain

1. Blind luck cannot be influenced
2. But fortune-favors-the-bold luck, prepared-mind luck, and destiny luck can all be cultivated
3. Pursuing all three relentlessly increases exposure to good outcomes and reduces dependence on fortune
4. Even without finding the big break, reversion to the mean means outcomes eventually track actual talent
5. Therefore persistent action across luck types is a reliable strategy for neutralizing bad luck
