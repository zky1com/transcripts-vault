# Orlando Bravo - Private equity creates value when it turns great innovators into disciplined businesses

## Position
Orlando Bravo says the best private equity firms create value by turning strong innovators into strong businesses through operational discipline, not by treating finance alone as the product.

## Evidence Cited
- He says his firm is in the business of turning great innovators into great businesses.
- He frames private equity as a change agent for companies that need a new operating approach.
- He repeatedly emphasizes management quality, incentives, and execution discipline.

## Logic Chain
1. Bravo does not describe value creation as merely buying cheap assets and levering them.
2. He focuses on changing how the company is run so the business quality improves.
3. That supports the claim that disciplined business building is the core PE edge.
