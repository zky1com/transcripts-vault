# Naval - If you can both sell and build you will be unstoppable

## Position
The most powerful combination in any industry is someone who can both build the product and sell it. Building includes engineering, design, manufacturing, and operations. Selling includes marketing, communicating, recruiting, fundraising, and PR. The classic Silicon Valley pattern pairs a world-class builder with a world-class seller (Jobs/Wozniak, Gates/Allen, Bezos). When one person can do both, they can create entire industries. This combination is what investors look for and what produces the greatest leverage.
