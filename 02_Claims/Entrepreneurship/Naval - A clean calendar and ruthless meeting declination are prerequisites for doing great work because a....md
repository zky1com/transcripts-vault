## Logic Chain

1. Deep creative and strategic work requires extended periods of uninterrupted focus
2. Meetings fragment time into unusable chunks and prime the mind with others' priorities
3. Each accepted meeting imposes a coordination overhead that extends well beyond its duration (preparation, follow-up, context switching)
4. Therefore: declining meetings is not rudeness but the structural requirement for high-quality output
5. The goal is not an empty calendar but a calendar where every hour is controlled by you for your highest-impact work
