- Four-question filter: is it true? true outside this context? true in my context? do I want to apply it?

## Logic Chain

1. Each person's advice is calibrated to their own circumstances, not yours
2. Multiple advisors with different circumstances give contradictory recommendations
3. Without your own model, you have no basis for choosing between contradictory recommendations
4. With your own model, each piece of advice becomes testable against your specific situation
5. Therefore: building your own model is the prerequisite for advice being useful, not a substitute for seeking advice
