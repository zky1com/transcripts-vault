# Sundar Pichai - AI Overviews are expanding query behavior and driving search growth where they appear

## Position
Sundar Pichai says AI Overviews are broadening the kinds of queries users make and are associated with search growth on the queries where they trigger.

## Evidence Cited
- He says AI Overviews are used by more than 1.5 billion users across over 150 countries.
- He says the nature of queries has expanded and that people now ask new kinds of questions.
- He says that on queries where AI Overviews trigger, Google sees query growth that continues over time.

## Logic Chain
1. Sundar points to large scale adoption of AI Overviews.
2. He ties that adoption to broader and growing search behavior.
3. Therefore the claim records his view that AI Overviews are additive to search usage.
