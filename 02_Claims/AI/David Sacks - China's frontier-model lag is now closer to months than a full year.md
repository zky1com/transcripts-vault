# David Sacks - China's frontier-model lag is now closer to months than a full year

## Position
DeepSeek's timeline suggests China's top-model lag to U.S. frontier reasoning models is now measured in months.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says prior consensus was roughly 6-12 months and shifted to about 3-6 months after R1.
- Discussion compares R1 with OpenAI 01 timing as a short gap.
- Panel treats the timing as meaningful capability convergence.

## Logic Chain
1. Comparable capability appearing quickly implies a smaller lag.
2. Short lag indicates faster catch-up at the frontier.
3. Therefore planning assumptions based on a one-year gap are weaker.
