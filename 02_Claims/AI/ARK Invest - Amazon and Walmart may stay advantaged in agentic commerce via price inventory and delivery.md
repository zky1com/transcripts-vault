# ARK Invest - Amazon and Walmart may stay advantaged in agentic commerce via price inventory and delivery

## Position
The panel suggests agentic interfaces may increase competition but still preserve structural advantages for scaled logistics platforms.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript repeatedly highlights three durable pillars: pricing power, inventory depth, and delivery speed.
- Speakers argue Amazon and Walmart's physical infrastructure supports those pillars under new AI interfaces.
- Discussion frames AI layers as potentially increasing power-law concentration among top operators.

## Logic Chain
1. Agentic discovery changes interface entry points, not fulfillment economics.
2. Fulfillment economics remain driven by cost, selection, and speed.
3. Therefore top logistics networks can remain advantaged even with new front-end agents.
