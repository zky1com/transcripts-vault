# 
Satya Nadella - Enterprise digital coworkers require identity and permission controls

## Position
Digital employee agents need enterprise identity, permissions, and traceability controls to be deployable at scale.

## Evidence Cited
- Nadella says agent deployment is about credentials and identity.
- He highlights permissions and decision-making boundaries as core controls.
- He emphasizes provenance questions like who did what to whom.

## Logic Chain
1. Enterprise automation must be auditable and attributable.
2. Unscoped agent actions create governance and security failures.
3. Therefore identity and permission controls are prerequisite infrastructure.
