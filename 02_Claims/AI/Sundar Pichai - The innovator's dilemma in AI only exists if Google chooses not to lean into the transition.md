# Sundar Pichai - The innovator's dilemma in AI only exists if Google chooses not to lean into the transition

## Position
Sundar Pichai says the innovator's dilemma only becomes real if Google refuses to lean into the AI transition as aggressively as users require.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the dilemma only exists if you treat it as a dilemma.
- He says in technology you must lean into massive waves of innovation as hard as you can.
- He compares the current moment with prior platform shifts such as mobile and Shorts where Google followed users first and monetization later.

## Logic Chain
1. Sundar frames platform transitions as moments for aggressive adaptation.
2. He rejects defensive hesitation as the right response.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view that Google must lean into AI instead of protecting legacy form factors.
