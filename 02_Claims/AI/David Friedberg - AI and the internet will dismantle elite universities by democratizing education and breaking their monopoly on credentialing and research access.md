# David Friedberg - AI and the internet will dismantle elite universities by democratizing education and breaking their monopoly on credentialing and research access

## Position
Elite universities have historically derived their value from two monopolies: exclusive access to knowledge and exclusive access to research funding infrastructure. The internet already democratized access to knowledge. AI will now democratize high-quality personalized tutoring at zero marginal cost — eliminating both monopolies and making the premium valuation of elite institutions unjustifiable.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "The internet democratized access to information and knowledge. You can watch MIT graduate courses. All of the core educational content that is delivered in prestigious higher education institutions has been largely democratized."
- Transcript: "AI is the next leg of the stool to break. We could see kids in Africa and kids in South Asia getting the equivalent of a Harvard graduate school degree at a cost of zero through personalized tutoring enabled through AI."
- Transcript: "There are independent research institutions that get research funding... it doesn't necessarily need to be within an educational framework."

## Logic Chain
1. Elite universities serve two functions: exclusive knowledge delivery and research facilitation.
2. The internet already broke the knowledge delivery monopoly by making all core content free.
3. AI will break personalized tutoring quality by delivering elite-level education at zero cost globally.
4. Research funding is migrating to independent institutions not tied to universities.
5. With both functions disrupted, the economic justification for elite university premiums collapses.
