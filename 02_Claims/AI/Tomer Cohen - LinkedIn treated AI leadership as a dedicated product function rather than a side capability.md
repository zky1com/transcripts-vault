# Tomer Cohen - LinkedIn treated AI leadership as a dedicated product function rather than a side capability

## Position

- Discussion connects this role directly to feed strategy and product build decisions.

## Logic Chain
1. Dedicated ownership increases strategic coherence in cross-functional AI deployment.
2. Without explicit ownership, AI initiatives fragment across teams and lose product focus.
3. Therefore role-level AI leadership can improve speed and consistency of platform transformation.
