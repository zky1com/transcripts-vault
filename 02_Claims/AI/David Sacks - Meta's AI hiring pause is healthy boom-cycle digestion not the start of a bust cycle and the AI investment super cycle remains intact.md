# David Sacks - Meta's AI hiring pause is healthy boom-cycle digestion not the start of a bust cycle and the AI investment super cycle remains intact

## Position
Meta's hiring freeze after an aggressive AI talent acquisition blitz reflects normal consolidation after rapid expansion, not a signal that the AI investment cycle is turning. The current correction in AI sentiment is healthy because it is deflating overhyped expectations (instant AGI, recursive self-improvement) while the fundamental AI investment super cycle continues.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "What Meta is doing is probably digesting a little bit. They've now made a bunch of talent acquisitions... and they're probably just consolidating a little bit. I don't think this is the bust part of the cycle."
- Transcript: "I actually think that we're still probably early to the middle of this investment super cycle. And it's just a healthy correction in sentiment here that people are realizing it's going to be a little bit harder."

## Logic Chain
1. Meta executed a compressed AI talent blitz (Daniel Gross, Scale AI team, hundreds of millions in offers).
2. A hiring pause follows logically as Meta integrates acquired talent and assesses ROI.
3. Organizational digestion after rapid expansion is a standard cycle in tech, not a reversal signal.
4. The underlying drivers of AI investment (fundamental capability improvements, competitive pressure, platform potential) remain intact.
