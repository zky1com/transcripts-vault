All-In Podcast - Large incumbents may compress paid chatbot economics through aggressive free distribution

## Position
The panel argues that ad-funded and cash-rich incumbents can pressure subscription economics by making comparable AI access broadly free.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript discusses risk that key consumer AI access points move to free pricing.
- Hosts compare potential path to historical browser/search monetization shifts.

## Logic Chain
1. Free distribution by dominant channels can reduce willingness to pay for substitutes.
2. Sustained subsidy can force margin compression in standalone paid offerings.
3. Therefore subscription-heavy challengers face pricing pressure if incumbents push free tiers aggressively.
