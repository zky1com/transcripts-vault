# David Friedberg - Shifting to small specialized language models will reduce AI energy and cost per token by 10-100x

## Position
Re-architecting AI around small, specialized models rather than monolithic large models will make systems 10x-100x more energy and cost efficient per token — causing token production to increase dramatically rather than decrease as the industry matures.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript: "This rearchitecturing dramatically reduces the energy cost and dollar cost per token. The systems could become 10x-100x more efficient."
- Transcript: "At that point token production will go way up, not way down."
- Transcript: Contextualized against the current "AI bubble" framing — Friedberg argues the efficiency improvements undercut the bubble narrative because costs will fall sharply.

## Logic Chain
1. Current frontier LLMs are large, general-purpose, and computationally expensive.
2. Specialized small models can perform specific tasks with far less compute.
3. Architectural optimization and specialization compound to deliver 10-100x efficiency gains.
4. Therefore total token output will scale dramatically upward as unit costs fall.
