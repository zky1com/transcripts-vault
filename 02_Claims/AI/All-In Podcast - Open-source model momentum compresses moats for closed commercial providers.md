# All-In Podcast - Open-source model momentum compresses moats for closed commercial providers

## Position
Rapid open-model progress can shorten durability of closed-model feature and pricing advantages.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript repeatedly compares open and closed model positioning.
- Panel discusses cost/performance pressure from open alternatives.
- Conversation links open diffusion to weaker lock-in assumptions.

## Logic Chain
1. Open alternatives improve substitution power for users.
2. Higher substitution pressure limits closed-provider pricing power.
3. So closed moats compress faster under strong open momentum.
