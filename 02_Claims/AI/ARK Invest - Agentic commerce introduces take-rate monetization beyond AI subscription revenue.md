# ARK Invest - Agentic commerce introduces take-rate monetization beyond AI subscription revenue

## Position
The discussion argues agentic checkout flows could create transaction take-rate revenue in addition to subscription fees.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript cites OpenAI integrations with Shopify and Etsy and an agentic commerce protocol with Stripe.
- Speakers describe monetization shifting from subscription-only models toward take rates on goods sold.
- Panel quantifies large addressable commerce spend and potential revenue from low single-digit take rates.

## Logic Chain
1. Subscription ceilings constrain per-user monetization in consumer AI.
2. Transaction rails let platforms monetize purchase flow through take rates.
3. Hence agentic commerce can become a second major revenue layer.
