# 
All-In Podcast - US AI competitiveness is framed as inseparable from industrial deployment and small-business diffusion, not only frontier-model leadership

## Position
The panel presents AI race outcomes as dependent on broad domestic deployment in manufacturing and small-business operations, not just lab-level model performance.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript discusses AI competitiveness in relation to onshoring, manufacturing, and small-business adoption.
- Panel links national advantage to applied deployment breadth.
- Hosts frame diffusion as a practical competitiveness metric.

## Logic Chain
1. Model leadership without deployment has limited macro effect.
2. Wide deployment creates compounding productivity and ecosystem effects.
3. Therefore competitiveness depends on diffusion into real operating sectors.
