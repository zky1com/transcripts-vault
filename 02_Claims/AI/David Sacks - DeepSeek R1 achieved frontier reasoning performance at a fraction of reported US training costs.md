# David Sacks - DeepSeek R1 achieved frontier reasoning performance at a fraction of reported US training costs

## Position
DeepSeek claimed to train its R1 reasoning model for approximately $6 million on 2,000 GPUs, matching OpenAI's o1-class performance while OpenAI reportedly spent $800 million to train GPT-4. This places DeepSeek's reported training cost at roughly 7% of the US frontier benchmark, challenging assumptions that AI leadership requires massive capital deployment.
