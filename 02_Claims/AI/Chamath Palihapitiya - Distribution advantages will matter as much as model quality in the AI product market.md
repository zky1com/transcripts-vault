# Chamath Palihapitiya - Distribution advantages will matter as much as model quality in the AI product market

## Position
Chamath argues that distribution will be decisive in AI competition, not just raw model quality.

## Evidence Cited
- He says "distribution still matters a ton."
- He says that dynamic favors Google and still favors OpenAI because of installed user reach.
- He frames model competition as converging with broader product and consumer-discovery dynamics.

## Logic Chain

2. He ties that advantage to large user bases and product surfaces.
3. Therefore the claim records his view that winner economics depend on distribution as much as model quality.
