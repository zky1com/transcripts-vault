# 
Satya Nadella - Enterprise AI adoption is occurring through both top-down ROI programs and bottom-up automation

## Position
Real enterprise AI diffusion is dual-track: leadership-directed ROI projects and grassroots automation by operating teams.

## Evidence Cited
- Nadella explicitly says adoption is both top-down and bottom-up.
- He cites top-down domains like customer service, supply chain, and HR.
- He gives bottom-up examples of teams building agents to remove devops drudgery.

## Logic Chain
1. Executive sponsorship unlocks budget and strategic prioritization.
2. Frontline teams identify high-friction workflows fastest.
3. So broad adoption requires both channels to run simultaneously.
