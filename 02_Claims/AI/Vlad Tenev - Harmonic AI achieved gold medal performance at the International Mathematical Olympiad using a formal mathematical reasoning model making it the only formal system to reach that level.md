## Logic Chain
1. IMO is the most prestigious international mathematics competition — gold medal level is the top tier.
2. Most AI models solving math use informal (natural language) proofs which can hallucinate without verification.
3. Formal models use proof verifiers that precisely confirm correctness — providing clean reward signals for RL training.
4. Harmonic's formal approach achieving gold means it can verify its own solutions, solving the hallucination problem for mathematical reasoning.
