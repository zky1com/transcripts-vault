# Geoffrey Hinton - AI can deliver enormous benefits while still carrying non-trivial takeover risk

## Position
Hinton presents a dual thesis: AI can produce major societal gains but also introduces meaningful existential control risk.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says AI will do enormous good while warning machines could take over.
- Interview repeatedly holds benefit and risk in parallel rather than choosing one narrative.
- The discussion questions whether humanity currently understands the implications well enough.

## Logic Chain
1. Technologies can be simultaneously high-upside and high-risk.
2. Policy failure often comes from treating these properties as mutually exclusive.
3. Therefore governance should optimize both innovation and risk containment, not one alone.
