# David Sacks - Reasoning models decompose complex problems via chain-of-thought unlike base LLMs

## Position
David Sacks explains that reasoning models represent a qualitatively different AI architecture from base language models. 3. Chain-of-thought enables decomposition of multi-step problems into ordered sub-tasks.
