# David Sacks - China-led open-source frontier models are reshaping AI competition

## Position
A growing share of leading open-source models coming from China is changing AI competition by accelerating model diffusion and reducing closed-model defensibility.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript explicitly says many leading open-source models are now coming from China and cites DeepSeek as a key example.
- The panel discusses open-source distribution as a force that spreads capability quickly beyond a few closed providers.
- Conversation frames this trend as strategic competition, not only technical experimentation.

## Logic Chain
1. Open-source release shortens the time from frontier development to broad adoption.
2. If China-origin models dominate open releases, China's influence on global model stacks increases.
3. That dynamic reshapes competitive moats for closed model providers and national AI positioning.
