# Dario Amodei - AI capability acceleration requires stronger governance before full autonomy arrives

## Position
Because capability is advancing quickly, governance and control frameworks need to mature ahead of highly autonomous model performance.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript repeatedly pairs near-term capability gains with concern about alignment and oversight readiness.
- The segment frames autonomy as approaching faster than many public institutions are prepared to regulate.
- Anthropic's own safety organization is presented as evidence that governance must scale with capability.

## Logic Chain
1. Governance lag creates a window where capability outpaces controls.
2. That lag raises systemic risk even if current systems are manageable.
3. Therefore governance investment must be front-loaded, not deferred.
