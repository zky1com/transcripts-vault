All-In Podcast - Workforce impact framing is shifting from pure displacement narratives toward role transformation and task reallocation

## Position
The episode argues labor outcomes are likely to center on role redesign and leverage expansion before full replacement in many domains.

## Evidence Cited

- Panel references transitions in task mix rather than immediate full elimination.

## Logic Chain
1. Technology adoption often first changes task composition within roles.
2. Role redesign can precede or offset outright headcount displacement in many sectors.
3. So workforce effects are likely to include substantial transformation dynamics.
