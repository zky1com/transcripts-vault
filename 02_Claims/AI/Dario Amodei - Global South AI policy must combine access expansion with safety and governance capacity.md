# Dario Amodei - Global South AI policy must combine access expansion with safety and governance capacity

## Position
AI strategy for the Global South should balance broad access and economic opportunity with concrete investment in governance capacity.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript emphasizes both growth opportunities and ethical questions as urgency increases.
- Speech context at India summit frames inclusion and safety as parallel objectives.
- Remarks present development framing that avoids pure acceleration without safeguards.

## Logic Chain
1. Access without safeguards can amplify harm and institutional stress.
2. Safeguards without access can block development gains and competitiveness.
3. Thus durable policy design requires simultaneous progress on both dimensions.
