# 
Satya Nadella - AI product teams are collapsing role boundaries into broader full-stack builder scopes

## Position
AI-era product development is restructuring teams by combining previously distinct product design frontend scopes into broader builder roles.

## Evidence Cited
- Nadella says LinkedIn combined PM, design, and frontend-style scopes into full-stack builders.
- He links this to a new eval-to-science-to-infrastructure loop.
- He frames this as structural workflow change, not just tool usage.

## Logic Chain
1. AI tools reduce friction between adjacent product roles.
2. Reduced handoffs can increase throughput and ownership clarity.
3. Therefore organizations are redesigning roles around end-to-end builder execution.
