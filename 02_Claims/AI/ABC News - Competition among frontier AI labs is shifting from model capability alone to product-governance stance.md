# ABC News - Competition among frontier AI labs is shifting from model capability alone to product-governance stance

## Position
Frontier AI competition is increasingly defined by product-governance choices such as monetization policy, safety posture, and user trust framing.

- Coverage frames governance stance as part of the commercial contest, not separate from it.

## Logic Chain

3. Thus product-governance strategy becomes a material competitive variable.
