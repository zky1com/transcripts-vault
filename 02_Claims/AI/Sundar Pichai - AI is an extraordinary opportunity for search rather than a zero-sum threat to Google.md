# Sundar Pichai - AI is an extraordinary opportunity for search rather than a zero-sum threat to Google

## Position
Sundar Pichai says AI is a major opportunity for search and does not view the transition as a zero-sum threat to Google.

## Evidence Cited
- He says he has viewed this period as an extraordinary opportunity for search.
- He says the amount of information people can access is expanding, not shrinking.
- He says the framing feels far from a zero-sum construct to him.

## Logic Chain
1. Sundar treats AI as an expansion of search usefulness.
2. He rejects the idea that the new interface necessarily destroys Google's position.
3. Therefore the claim captures his view that AI can strengthen search rather than simply cannibalize it.
