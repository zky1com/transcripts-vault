# David Sacks - The AI market currently has five major competitors with distinct product strengths

## Position
David Sacks says the AI market is currently competitive across five major firms, each with a different strength rather than one universal lead.

## Evidence Cited
- He says "we've got five major companies."
- He assigns consumer leadership to ChatGPT, enterprise strength to Anthropic, and current-events strength to xAI.
- He says Meta has hit headwinds but will keep investing and can come back.

## Logic Chain
1. Sacks enumerates a multi-firm competitive field.
2. He differentiates the firms by capability or distribution niche.
3. Therefore the claim records his view that AI remains a diversified competitive market.
