# 
Satya Nadella - AI competitiveness depends on diffusion and ecosystem effects, not benchmarks alone

## Position
AI leadership should be assessed by deployment intensity and ecosystem formation across sectors, not by model metrics alone.

## Evidence Cited
- Nadella says technology benefits come from intense real-world use.
- He argues diffusion across sectors is the key phase after model creation.
- He highlights platform ecosystem employment effects as a major success signal.

## Logic Chain
1. Benchmarks do not guarantee economic adoption.
2. Ecosystem participation multiplies impact beyond first-party products.
3. So diffusion and ecosystem metrics are core competitiveness indicators.
