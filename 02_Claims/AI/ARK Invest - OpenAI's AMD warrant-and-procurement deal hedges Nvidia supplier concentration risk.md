# ARK Invest - OpenAI's AMD warrant-and-procurement deal hedges Nvidia supplier concentration risk

## Position
The panel argues OpenAI's AMD warrants plus large chip commitments reduce single-vendor dependence on Nvidia.

## Evidence Cited
- Transcript says OpenAI is investing in AMD through warrants while committing to buy large volumes of AMD chips.
- Discussion explicitly frames the move as reducing OpenAI's single-supplier risk to Nvidia.
- Speakers describe AMD chips as increasingly competitive for inference workloads.

## Logic Chain
1. If OpenAI secures large AMD capacity and equity exposure, concentration risk to one chip vendor falls.
2. Inference competition between AMD and Nvidia increases optionality for model serving.
3. Therefore the deal functions as a strategic hedge, not only a financing event.
