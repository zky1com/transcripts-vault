# Dave Ricks - The next major upside for GLP-1 science may come from addiction and mental-health applications, not just obesity

## Position
Dave Ricks says one of the most promising future directions for GLP-1-related science is in addiction and mental-health conditions, where early evidence suggests the biology may matter well beyond obesity.

## Evidence Cited
- He discusses evidence around smoking, gambling, and other compulsive behaviors changing under these medicines.
- He says Lilly is starting studies in bipolar disorder and major depressive disorder.
- He frames these effects as emerging discoveries rather than the original design target.

## Logic Chain
1. Ricks distinguishes intended weight-loss use from newly observed behavioral and psychiatric effects.
2. He is committing actual study resources to those adjacent indications.
3. That supports the claim that addiction and mental health may be a major next frontier.
