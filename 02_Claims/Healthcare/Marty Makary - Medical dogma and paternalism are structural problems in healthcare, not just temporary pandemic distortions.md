# Marty Makary - Medical dogma and paternalism are structural problems in healthcare, not just temporary pandemic distortions

## Position
Marty Makary says medical dogma and paternalism are persistent structural problems inside healthcare and should not be understood as one-off distortions caused only by the pandemic era.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the sociology of medical dogma is a long-running phenomenon.
- He treats paternalism as a recurring structural problem.
- He links his FDA role to correcting those patterns rather than just cleaning up one episode.

## Logic Chain
1. Makary describes these issues as deep institutional habits.
2. He is not limiting the critique to COVID-era excesses.
3. That supports the claim that dogma and paternalism are structural problems.
