# Dave Ricks - A transformational drug category will always trigger intense pricing and access pressure before health systems fully adapt

## Position
Dave Ricks says breakthrough therapies like GLP-1s inevitably create immediate pricing and access pressure because demand outruns the ability of existing healthcare systems and reimbursement rules to adapt.

## Evidence Cited
- He says breakthroughs create pricing and access pressure immediately.
- He explains that insurance coverage has lagged the real therapeutic impact of the drugs.
- He notes that huge demand and political scrutiny arrived quickly as the class succeeded.

## Logic Chain
1. Ricks ties pressure not just to price but to a mismatch between therapeutic value and system readiness.
2. He argues that this mismatch is a natural consequence of a genuinely transformative category.
3. That supports the claim that pricing pressure is structural when a breakthrough arrives.
