# Mark Cuban - Radical price transparency can expose how irrational medicine markups have become

## Position
Mark Cuban argues that radical price transparency reveals how irrational medication markups have become and is the fastest way to pressure the system.

## Evidence Cited
- He says Cost Plus Drugs publishes actual acquisition cost, its markup, shipping, and pharmacy-review fee.
- He contrasts that transparent price structure with cases where patients are quoted hundreds or thousands of dollars for generic drugs.
- He says almost no other participant in the market openly publishes comparable pricing.

## Logic Chain
1. Cuban believes hidden pricing protects bad actors from scrutiny.
2. He argues that publishing the full cost stack makes the distortions legible to patients and employers.
3. That supports the claim that radical transparency exposes irrational drug markups.
