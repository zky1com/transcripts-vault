# Doug Burgum - America should pursue energy dominance because AI manufacturing and allied energy security all depend on abundant power

## Position
Doug Burgum argues that energy dominance matters because the United States needs abundant power for AI, reindustrialization, and supplying allies so they do not depend on adversaries.

## Evidence Cited
- He says energy dominance is about powering AI in America, powering remanufacturing, and selling energy to friends and allies.
- He distinguishes energy dominance from mere energy independence.
- He frames energy supply as a strategic capability rather than just a commodity business.

## Logic Chain
1. Burgum ties power demand directly to AI and industrial capacity.
2. He also links US exports to allied security and reduced adversary leverage.
3. So the claim captures his view that energy dominance is a national strategy, not just an industry slogan.
