# Doug Burgum - The AI arms race is fundamentally constrained by access to electricity and China is moving faster than the United States

## Position
Doug Burgum says the decisive bottleneck in the AI race is electricity and warns that China is rapidly expanding power capacity far faster than the United States.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the AI arms race is really driven by access to electricity.
- He highlights China adding enormous coal-powered capacity in a single year.
- He presents power buildout as the strategic factor that keeps him up at night.

## Logic Chain
1. Burgum treats compute growth and industrial scale as downstream of power availability.
2. He contrasts China's rapid additions with slower American buildout.
3. So the claim reflects his warning that electricity access is the core strategic constraint.
