# Doug Burgum - America is sabotaging itself by making critical-mineral and copper projects take decades to permit

## Position
Doug Burgum says the United States is undermining its own industrial and national-security position by letting mine approvals drag on for decades.

## Evidence Cited
- He cites a major copper project that has taken roughly 29 years to move through the process.
- He says the country needs domestic mining and refining for critical minerals.
- He presents the permitting system as a competitive handicap versus China.

## Logic Chain
1. Burgum links critical minerals to defense, manufacturing, and energy technology.
2. He says the current permitting timeline is incompatible with those strategic needs.
3. So the claim captures his view that the system amounts to self-inflicted sabotage.
