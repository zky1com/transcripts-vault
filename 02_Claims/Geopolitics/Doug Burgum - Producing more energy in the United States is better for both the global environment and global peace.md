# Doug Burgum - Producing more energy in the United States is better for both the global environment and global peace

## Position
Doug Burgum says the world is better off when the United States produces more energy because American production is cleaner and it can displace adversaries that fund conflict with oil and gas revenue.

## Evidence Cited
- He says the world should want every liquid fuel molecule and electron produced in the United States.
- He argues the US produces energy cleaner, safer, and smarter than other countries.
- He says replacing adversary energy sales cuts funding for wars and terrorism.

## Logic Chain
1. Burgum connects American energy production to lower relative environmental damage.
2. He separately connects it to weakening geopolitical adversaries that rely on resource revenues.
3. That supports the claim that more US production helps both climate tradeoffs and peace.
