# Microsoft annual reports show revenue grew from 168 billion in fiscal 2021 to 281.7 billion in fiscal 2025 while headcount rose from 181000 to 228000

## Fact
- Microsoft reported revenue of $168 billion in FY2021 and $281.7 billion in FY2025, while full-time headcount rose from about 181,000 to about 228,000 over the same endpoints.

## Source Excerpt
- AR2021 and AR2025 list the cited revenue and full-time employee figures in shareholder and human-capital sections.
