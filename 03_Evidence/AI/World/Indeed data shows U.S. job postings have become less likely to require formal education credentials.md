# Indeed data shows U.S. job postings have become less likely to require formal education credentials

## Fact
- Indeed Hiring Lab reports that the share of U.S. job postings mentioning formal education requirements has declined and many postings no longer include a degree requirement.

## Source Excerpt
- The analysis highlights a multi-year trend toward skills-based hiring language over formal credential filters.
