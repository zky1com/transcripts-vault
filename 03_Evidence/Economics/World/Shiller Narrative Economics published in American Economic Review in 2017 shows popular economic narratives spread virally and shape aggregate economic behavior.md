# Shiller Narrative Economics published in American Economic Review in 2017 shows popular economic narratives spread virally and shape aggregate economic behavior

## Core Evidence
Robert Shiller's "Narrative Economics" was published in the American Economic Review, vol. 107(4), pp. 967–1004, in 2017. It was delivered as his presidential address at the 129th AEA annual meeting and argues that contagious popular narratives — not just economic fundamentals — drive major fluctuations in aggregate economic behavior and asset prices.
