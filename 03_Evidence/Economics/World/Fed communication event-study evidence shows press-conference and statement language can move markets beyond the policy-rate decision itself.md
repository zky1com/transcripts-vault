# Fed communication event-study evidence shows press-conference and statement language can move markets beyond the policy-rate decision itself

## Fact
- Event studies around FOMC announcements indicate that communication components, including press conferences, contribute materially to asset-price reactions in addition to target-rate changes.

## Source Excerpt
- Federal Reserve research documents a larger share of market response attributable to communication windows in recent years.
