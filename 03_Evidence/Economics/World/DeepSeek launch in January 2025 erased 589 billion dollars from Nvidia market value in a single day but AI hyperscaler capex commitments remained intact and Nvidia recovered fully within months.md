# DeepSeek launch in January 2025 erased 589 billion dollars from Nvidia market value in a single day but AI hyperscaler capex commitments remained intact and Nvidia recovered fully within months

## Core Evidence
On January 27, 2025, Chinese AI startup DeepSeek released a free, open-source large language model claiming to have been developed in just two months at a cost of under $6 million. The release triggered immediate concerns about the economics of AI infrastructure investment, causing Nvidia stock to fall 16.9% — erasing a record $589 billion in market capitalization in a single day. However, the shock did not cause hyperscalers to reduce their AI capital expenditure commitments: Meta, Microsoft, Amazon, and Alphabet went on to commit approximately $475 billion in combined 2026 capex. Nvidia fully recovered by January 2026. This episode directly illustrates the claim's thesis: AI-themed markets can experience abrupt confidence shocks from incremental competitive or technological developments, while the underlying multi-year infrastructure investment cycle remains intact.
