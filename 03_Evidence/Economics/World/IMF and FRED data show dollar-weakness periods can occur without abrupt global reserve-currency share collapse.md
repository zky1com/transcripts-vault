# IMF and FRED data show dollar-weakness periods can occur without abrupt global reserve-currency share collapse

## Fact
- IMF COFER data shows the U.S. dollar remains the largest global reserve currency by share, while FRED broad dollar series show cyclical multi-quarter weakening episodes.

## Source Excerpt
- Reserve-share persistence and cyclical FX movement can coexist, supporting non-binary interpretation of dollar weakness.
