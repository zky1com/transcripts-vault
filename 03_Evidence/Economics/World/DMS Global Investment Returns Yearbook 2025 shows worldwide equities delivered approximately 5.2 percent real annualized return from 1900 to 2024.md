# DMS Global Investment Returns Yearbook 2025 shows worldwide equities delivered approximately 5.2 percent real annualized return from 1900 to 2024

## Core Evidence
The Dimson, Marsh, and Staunton (DMS) 2025 Global Investment Returns Yearbook, published by UBS, reports that worldwide equities delivered approximately 5.2% real annualized return from 1900 to 2024 across 35 markets — consistent with the long-run resilience thesis invoked in crash-context commentary.

## External Source
- https://www.ubs.com/global/en/investment-bank/insights-and-data/2025/global-investment-returns-yearbook-2025.html

## Verification Notes
- Manually checked online on 2026-02-26; the 5.2% real global equity return figure for 1900-2024 is stated in the 2025 Yearbook summary.

## Limits
- The 5.2% figure is for the full worldwide equity index; individual market results vary substantially and some markets experienced total loss periods.
