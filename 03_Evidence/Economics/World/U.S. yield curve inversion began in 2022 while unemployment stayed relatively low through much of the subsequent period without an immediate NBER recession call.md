# U.S. yield curve inversion began in 2022 while unemployment stayed relatively low through much of the subsequent period without an immediate NBER recession call

## Fact
- FRED data shows sustained 10y-2y inversion after 2022 while unemployment remained comparatively low for an extended period and NBER did not immediately date a new recession.

## Source Excerpt
- The series and business-cycle chronology illustrate that timing between inversion signals and recession dating can vary materially.
