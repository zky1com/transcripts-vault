# PWL Canada 2005-2024 report finds renting and owning outcomes are often comparable across major cities

## Fact
- The PWL 2005-2024 Canada analysis reports mixed city outcomes and no automatic winner between renting and owning under its modeling assumptions.

## Source Excerpt
- The report shows renter-to-owner wealth outcomes vary by city and period, with many periods where renting is competitive or superior.
