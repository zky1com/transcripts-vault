# NAR 2024 existing home sales data shows lowest annual turnover since 1995 as elevated mortgage rates suppressed existing-home transactions

## Core Evidence
The National Association of Realtors (NAR) reports that 2024 existing home sales totaled 4.06 million, the lowest annual level since 1995. The 2024 result was below the prior record low of 4.10 million set in 2023, with prolonged high mortgage rates discouraging homeowners from trading existing low-rate mortgages for new higher-rate loans — directly constraining housing-linked demand from home-improvement and furniture retailers.

## Limits
- Existing home sales are the primary metric; new home starts remained more resilient, and the constraint applies specifically to housing-linked retailers rather than homebuilders dependent on new construction.
