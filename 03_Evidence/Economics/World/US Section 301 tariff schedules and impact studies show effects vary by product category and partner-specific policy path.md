# US Section 301 tariff schedules and impact studies show effects vary by product category and partner-specific policy path

## Fact
- U.S. tariff programs apply different rates across product lists and time windows, and measured economic effects vary by sector and supply-chain exposure.

## Source Excerpt
- Official tariff program materials and retrospective analysis show non-uniform policy design and non-uniform impact magnitudes.
