# Fed funds futures and front-end yields show markets often price easing paths ahead of realized policy moves

## Fact
- CME FedWatch and front-end Treasury data show expected policy-rate paths can shift materially before actual FOMC cuts, affecting discount rates for rate-sensitive sectors.

## Source Excerpt
- Forward policy pricing in futures and 2-year yields often moves in advance of realized policy changes.
