# Historical ISM and broad-equity series show many drawdowns resolved as cyclical rotation rather than permanent regime break

## Fact
- In several cycles, equity drawdowns occurred during temporary macro stress and were followed by stabilization as activity indicators improved.

## Source Excerpt
- Combined ISM and broad-index time series show stepwise stress-and-recovery patterns rather than one-way structural breaks in many episodes.
