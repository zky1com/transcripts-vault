# PWL Canada 2005-2024 analysis reports renting and owning can be financially comparable rather than having an automatic winner

## Core Evidence
PWL's Canada 2005-2024 analysis reports mixed city outcomes and no automatic financial winner between renting and owning when full owner costs and renter investing are modeled.

## Limits
- This evidence supports comparability under explicit modeling assumptions, not a universal rule for all households.
