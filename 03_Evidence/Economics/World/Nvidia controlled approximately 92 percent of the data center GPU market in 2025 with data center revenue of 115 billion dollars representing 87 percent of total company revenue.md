# Nvidia controlled approximately 92 percent of the data center GPU market in 2025 with data center revenue of 115 billion dollars representing 87 percent of total company revenue

## Core Evidence
Nvidia controlled approximately 92% of the discrete GPU market in the first half of 2025, and maintained approximately 86% share of the broader AI data center revenue segment. Nvidia's data center revenues reached $115.2 billion in FY2025, a 142% year-over-year increase, representing 87% of Nvidia's total company revenue. The nearest competitor, AMD, generated approximately $2.3 billion in data center GPU revenue — roughly 2% of Nvidia's scale. This extreme supply concentration means that competitive access to AI compute is functionally gated through a single hardware vendor, making GPU allocation a strategic bottleneck for AI development teams as the claim describes.
