# FDIC Q4 2025 reports overall bank asset quality remained favorable while weakness persisted in select loan portfolios

## Fact
- FDIC's Q4 2025 profile says overall asset quality remained generally favorable, while non-owner-occupied CRE, multifamily CRE, auto, and credit-card portfolios showed persistent weakness.

## Source Excerpt
- The report states industry metrics were favorable overall but identified above-pre-pandemic stress in selected portfolios.
