# SEC investor bulletin says ETF investor costs can include trading spreads and tracking effects beyond expense ratios

## Core Evidence
SEC investor guidance notes that ETF ownership costs can include commissions, bid-ask spreads, and premium/discount effects in addition to the expense ratio.

## External Source
- https://www.sec.gov/oiea/investor-alerts-bulletins/ib_etfs

## Verification Notes
- Manually checked online on 2026-02-25; SEC source directly supports non-fee ETF cost components.

## Limits
- This evidence supports "fees are not the only cost" and does not by itself prove specific magnitudes.
