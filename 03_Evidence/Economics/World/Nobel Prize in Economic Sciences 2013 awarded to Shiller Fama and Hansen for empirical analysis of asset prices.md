# Nobel Prize in Economic Sciences 2013 awarded to Shiller Fama and Hansen for empirical analysis of asset prices

## Core Evidence
The 2013 Sveriges Riksbank Prize in Economic Sciences in Memory of Alfred Nobel was awarded jointly to Eugene F. Fama, Lars Peter Hansen, and Robert J. Shiller "for their empirical analysis of asset prices." Shiller's contribution highlighted that longer-term stock price movements can be partially predicted by valuation metrics such as the cyclically adjusted price-to-earnings ratio.

## Limits
- The prize was shared; Shiller's specific contribution was to long-run predictability and bubble research rather than Fama's efficient-markets work.
