## Limits
- Market size estimates vary significantly by methodology and scope; Juniper's $89T covers B2B specifically while McKinsey's $179T is total cross-border. The 6.35% cost figure applies to remittances (often consumer), which may be higher than enterprise B2B transaction costs. The market size itself is established but the exact "inefficiency" extraction opportunity for fintech is uncertain.
