# Real-yield regimes and gold market analysis support a constructive medium-term gold backdrop when real yields ease

## Fact
- Public real-yield data and gold-market analysis support that lower or easing real yields can improve the relative appeal of gold versus yield-bearing alternatives.

## Source Excerpt
- FRED publishes the U.S. 10-year TIPS real-yield series, while World Gold Council commentary explicitly frames higher real yields as downside pressure and easing yields as supportive for gold.
