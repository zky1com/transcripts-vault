# Global podcast listenership 584 million in 2025 and US cable TV penetration fell below 40 percent

## Core Evidence
Global podcast listenership reached approximately 584 million people in 2025, a 6.8% year-over-year increase, projected to exceed 650 million by 2027. In the US, 66% of consumers aged 12-34 had listened to a podcast in the last month (Edison Research 2025). Simultaneously, traditional cable TV household penetration in the US fell below 40% in 2025, down from approximately 50% just three years prior. Video podcasts are consumed on platforms like YouTube by one in three US podcast listeners. ## Limits
- Podcast growth does not directly measure book discovery channel shifts; the broader media fragmentation trend is well-documented but its specific impact on publishing demand curves requires additional category-level data. Cable TV decline reflects cord-cutting to streaming (Netflix, Disney+) as much as podcast/social shift.
