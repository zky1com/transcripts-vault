# CBOE buy-write research and fund disclosures support that distribution yield is not total return and covered calls can lag in strong equity markets

## Core Evidence
CBOE buy-write research describes the core tradeoff of covered-call strategies: option premium can cushion declines, but upside is truncated in strong bull markets. ETF/fund disclosure language also states distribution yield does not represent total return.
