# Historical macro series show market stress episodes have often overlapped with later stabilization when ISM and policy trajectories improved

## Fact
- FRED ISM PMI, policy-rate, and equity-index series show multiple cycles where near-term stress occurred before broader stabilization as activity indicators and policy expectations improved.

## Source Excerpt
- The joint behavior of ISM, policy rates, and broad equity index levels supports a staged transition rather than one-step regime shift in several cycles.
