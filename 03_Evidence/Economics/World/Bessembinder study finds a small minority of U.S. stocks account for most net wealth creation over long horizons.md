# Bessembinder study finds a small minority of U.S. stocks account for most net wealth creation over long horizons

## Fact
- Hendrik Bessembinder's long-run U.S. equity study reports that a small fraction of stocks explains essentially all net wealth creation above Treasury bills.

## Source Excerpt
- The paper shows highly skewed stock-level outcomes where aggregate wealth creation is concentrated in a limited subset of firms.
