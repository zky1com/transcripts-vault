# FDR 1937 letter states collective bargaining cannot be fully transplanted into public service

## Fact
- Franklin D. Roosevelt's August 16, 1937 letter states that collective bargaining as practiced in private industry cannot be transferred into public service in full form.

## Source Excerpt
- Letter text frames public employment as distinct from private-sector labor relations.
