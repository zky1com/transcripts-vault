# FDA adaptive-design guidance supports interim-informed trial modifications under prespecified rules

## Fact
- FDA guidance for drugs and biologics permits adaptive trial designs that use interim data to modify trial elements when prospectively planned and statistically controlled.

## Source Excerpt
- The guidance outlines requirements for preserving validity while using interim analyses and adaptations.
