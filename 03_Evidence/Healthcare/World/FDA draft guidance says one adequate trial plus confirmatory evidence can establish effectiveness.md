# FDA draft guidance says one adequate trial plus confirmatory evidence can establish effectiveness

## Fact
- FDA draft guidance states one adequate and well-controlled trial may be sufficient when supported by confirmatory evidence.

## Source Excerpt
- "one adequate and well-controlled trial and confirmatory evidence may be sufficient."
