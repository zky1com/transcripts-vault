# FDA general wellness policy defines a low-risk consumer lane separate from medical claims

## Fact
- FDA general wellness policy describes low-risk consumer products that are not actively regulated as medical devices when no disease-treatment claims are made.

## Source Excerpt
- The policy draws a boundary between general wellness claims and medical-grade diagnostic or treatment claims.
