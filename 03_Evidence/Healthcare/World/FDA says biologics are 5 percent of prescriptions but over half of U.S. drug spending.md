# FDA says biologics are 5 percent of prescriptions but over half of U.S. drug spending

## Fact
- FDA states biologics account for about 5% of prescriptions and more than half of U.S. drug spending.
- FDA also states biosimilars still represent less than 20% of the biologics market.
