# FDA says companies may self-determine GRAS status and are not required to notify the agency

## Fact
- FDA explains that manufacturers may independently conclude a substance is GRAS and are not required to submit a GRAS notice to FDA.

## Source Excerpt
- FDA states participation in the GRAS notification process is voluntary.
