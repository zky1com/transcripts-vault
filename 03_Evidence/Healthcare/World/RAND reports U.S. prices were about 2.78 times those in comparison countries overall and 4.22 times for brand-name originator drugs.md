# RAND reports U.S. prices were about 2.78 times those in comparison countries overall and 4.22 times for brand-name originator drugs

## Fact
- RAND found U.S. prescription drug prices were roughly 2.78x comparison-country levels overall and about 4.22x for brand-name originator products.

## Source Excerpt
- RAND's 2024 update compares U.S. prices with OECD peers and reports substantial U.S. price multiples.
